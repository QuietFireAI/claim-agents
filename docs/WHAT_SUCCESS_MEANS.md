# What Success Means - Insurance Claim Swarm

Per playbook: what completion delivers, what human work it displaces,
and what the human still owns. No invented metrics. The licensed
adjuster keeps every determination; the swarm keeps the clocks, the
records, and the grind.

## P01 - Tender... FNOL to Dispatch
**Delivers:** a captured-verbatim FNOL, assignment, policy facts with
system timestamps, inspection dispatched from the ratified panel.
**Displaces:** the intake scramble and the "was it even assigned?" gap.
**Human still owns:** every coverage question - policy facts are facts,
never statements.

## P02 - Track & Exception
**Delivers:** inspection results flowing to sourced estimates; every
number carries its source; exceptions surface, never smooth over.
**Human still owns:** valuation judgment beyond the sourced arithmetic.

## P03 - Paperwork Close
**Delivers:** a closure package - facts, estimate, file citations - with
the coverage-statement field hardcoded to None.
**Human still owns:** the closure decision itself, entirely.

## P04 - Detention/Vendor Cycle
**Delivers:** vendor dispatch from the ratified panel only; no panel
entry = no dispatch = human review (fail-closed).
**Human still owns:** panel membership and any off-panel exception.

## P05 - Fraud Signal Cycle
**Delivers:** silent aggregation ("handling_changed: False" ON the
referral record), SIU packages, and a guaranteed signed ending -
referral, cleared, or monitored. Delay-on-suspicion is the named unfair
practice this design makes structurally impossible.
**Human still owns:** every disposition, signed.

## P06 - Subrogation Preparation
**Delivers:** recovery packages with netting as arithmetic shown;
demands and acceptances move only on signed recovery.authority.
**Human still owns:** the negotiation - the swarm never negotiates.

## P07 - Payments & Reserves
**Delivers:** signed-only payments and reserve changes; books at $0.00 -
a one-cent variance is an exception to the human.
**Human still owns:** every dollar and every reserve, by signature.

## P08 - DOI Response
**Delivers:** inquiry packages to the human with clocks armed
(10-day lead, 3-day escalation, ratified 2026-07-18); the response
draft field is None - humans answer regulators.
**Human still owns:** the response, word for word.

## P09/P10 - Morning/EOD Books
**Delivers:** every wait named with age and blocking party; every live
clock listed. **Human still owns:** the priorities.

## P11 - Representation Halt
**Delivers:** attorney language detected at FNOL, in replies, or in
documents halts the claimant channel claim-wide SAME TURN; held
outbound becomes a visible wait; inbound is logged and routed, never
answered. **Human still owns:** everything after the halt.

## P12 - Policy Change Mid-Claim
**Delivers:** the change with both fact sets, blast radius from the
file, clocks re-derived, human notified - posted history never
rewritten. **Human still owns:** rescission and every coverage-adjacent
call.

## P13 - Salvage & Recovery Cascade
**Delivers:** salvage surfacing at valuation, folded into the recovery
package with sources; execution only on signature; proceeds reconcile
to $0.00. **Human still owns:** payee, amount, and acceptance.

## P14 - Records Request Response
**Delivers:** an itemized inventory (existence/type/date/source,
privilege-FLAGGED never privilege-judged) inside the response clock.
**Human still owns:** every release and every privilege call; legal
process is counsel's lane.

## The honest ledger
This swarm does not determine coverage, negotiate, adjudicate
privilege, respond to regulators, or move a cent unsigned. Every
sentence above is enforced in code and proven in tests_claim/ - the
suite is the claim.
